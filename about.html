About Creme de la Creme Bakery!

Creme de la Creme Bakery is a full-service Bakery with the first bakery built in 2009. It is located in the heart of Woodcrest, riverside. We also have a second bakery located in Corona, in the Shoppes of Dos Lagos. We are currently building our third location in East Riverside/Moreno Valley Area located at 2692 Canton Springs Parkway across from Rubios. We specialize in cakes for any occasion you can think of.

No two cakes are the same! Every custom cake that is made is unique to your taste. We offer a variety of buttercreams, whipped creams and Fondant.

As a full-service bakery, we bake a large variety of pastries and croissants, as well as fancy desserts like cannolis, napoleans, cream horns, cream puffs and eclairs.

We serve New York Bagels that we ship in on a monthly basis. These bagels are authentic and your first bite, you will taste the difference. We sell them fully cooked or frozen. Your choice!

Lastly, we serve mouth-watering sandwiches on our bread that we make fresh daily. We offer French baguettes, sourdough, rosemary-garlic sourdough, wheat, 7-grain, rye, brioche, Challah bread and a variety of other breads.
