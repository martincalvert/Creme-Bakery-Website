Ordering A Cake!

The following form will give us some of the key information we need to complete your cake order. This form is the starting point for your order, submission of this form does not confirm your order. Our cake department will contact you within 24 hours of receiving the below information to collect the remaining details and process your order. If you do not want to submit this form than you can also talk to our Cake Department to start your order Riverside Bakery location at (951) 780-9222 or email us at custombakeryorders@cremebakery.com. Please give us as much notice as possible for any and all custom orders. Depending on the intricacy of the order we typically do need at least a 4 day notice for anything custom, but we can always do something for you with a shorter notice as well. Thank you for your business and we look forward to speaking with you!
